Coffees

At !dea Cafe, we have an assortment of coffees from lattes, to espressos and cappuccinos. We also have longstanding favorites such as straight black as well as with cream and sugar. Come in and satisfy your caffeine cravings by which ever means suits your pallete.

Coffee Beans

!dea Cafe has a rich collection of varieties of coffee beans from all around the world. Our Baristas provide a wealth of information about beans from as far away as Marrakesh to Sao Paolo. We invite you to inquire about the origins of your cup of joe because we believe it provides for an even wholesome drinking experience.

Pastries

!dea Cafe's renowned pastry chefs entreat you to try one of our decadent treats. Go ahead, satisfy that sweet tooth with an Aloo Pie originally from Trinidad and Tobago or a Bakewell Pudding from the United Kingdom or some Baklava from Turkey, and not forgetting our famous good old-fashioned American Apple Pie. At !dea, the only thing sweeter than our pastries is the smile on our customers' faces after visiting the Cafe.
